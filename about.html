Home Owners Association Management
Southern Oregon's Premier HOA Management Company

Since Home Owners Association Management, LLC (HOAM) is Southern Oregon’s first management company to only serve HOA’s, it makes us the best fit to serve as your community manager.

Other management companies primarily focus on rental and/or sale services so HOA’s get the backseat on having well trained and knowledgeable community managers that effectively manage your community.

Whether you’re an already established HOA with a full board of directors, partial board or a developer with declarant rights, HOAM is the best choice for your association’s community management needs. HOAM also assists banks that have taken over for Declarants.

We serve the two types of associations that are recognized in the state of Oregon, Planned Unit Developments (PUDs) which are typically townhomes, multiplexes, and single family homes and Condominiums that are all residential or mixed in with commercial space.

Here at HOAM we are keen to common HOA issues. From enforcing association governing documents like the CC&Rs and Board Regulations to assisting boards formalize budgets to establish association dues.

HOAM is based out of Southern Oregon. With focus throughout Jackson County in cities like Medford, Ashland, Jacksonville, Eagle Point, Central Point, White City, Phoenix, Talent, Shady Cove, and more! We are also able to provide services throughout Oregon, so don’t hesitate to contact HOAM and find out how we can help your HOA today.
